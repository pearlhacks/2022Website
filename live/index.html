Check-In

How to Check In

Upon entering the FPG Student Union, please navigate to the great hall. Follow the signs to our check-in tables.
Have your student ID and COVID vaccination card / negative test ready to present at check-in. You will be asked to provide your full name and other information.

COVID Attestation

Make sure you understand our policies below and complete the COVID-19 Safety Attestation form BEFORE checking in at our in-person venue.

  • Covid Attestation Form: https://bit.ly/PH22COVID
  • Information about our Pearl Hacks 2022 COVID Policies

Venue Info

Location: Frank Porter Graham Student Union 209 South Road Chapel Hill, NC, 27599

Venue Map: Frank Porter Graham Student Union

Parking is free during the weekend at UNC Chapel Hill. We recommend parking at Cobb deck which is just a few minutes away from the Student Union.

What is Pearl Hacks?

Pearl Hacks is a hackathon for female and non-binary students. We especially cater to first-time hackers who are looking for a supportive environment to explore technology. Participants work in teams to create incredible projects in 24 hours with the help of excellent mentors, workshops, and tech talks. All the while, they’ll get to meet some fantastic people, enjoy fun games, collect swag, and eat free food.
